At Bakavathi Screen Prints

We don’t just print designs; we bring them to life. Since 2004, we’ve been a cornerstone of the screen printing industry, mastering the art of table printing and delivering unmatched quality that speaks for itself. With over two decades of expertise, we’ve built a legacy rooted in precision, creativity, and trust.

Located in the vibrant Tiruppur district of Tamil Nadu, the “Textile Capital of India,” we’re part of a city that thrives on innovation and craftsmanship. Tiruppur’s global reputation as a leader in knitwear production inspires us to set new benchmarks in screen printing, combining traditional techniques with modern technology to produce results that stand out.
